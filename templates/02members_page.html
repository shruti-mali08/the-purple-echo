  • RM aka. Kim Namjoon

    RM, whose full name is Kim Namjoon, is the leader, main rapper, songwriter, and producer under BigHit Music. Kim Namjoon was born in Ilsan, South Korea, on September 12, 1994.
    RM made his debut as a BTS member on June 13, 2013 with the release of BTS's first single album "2 Cool 4 Skool." In addition to leading BTS, RM has been heavily involved in writing and producing many of the group's songs, contributing to their lyrical depth and identity.


    1. "Do You" - from his first mixtape RM - March 20, 2015
    2. "Forever Rain" - from his second mixtape mono. - October 23, 2018
    3. "Wild Flower (with youjeen)" - from his first solo album Indigo - December 2, 2022
    4. "Still Life" - collaboration with Anderson .Paak - December 2, 2022
    5. "Come Back to Me" - pre-release single from his second solo album Right Place, Wrong Person - May 10, 2024

    RM is widely recognized for his intellectual lyricism and artistic direction. His works explore self-reflection, art, and humanity, marking him as one of the most influential figures in Korean hip-hop and pop music.


  • Jin aka. Kim Seokjin

    Jin, whose full name is Kim Seokjin, is the singer-songwriter, producer, actor under BigHit Music. Kim Seokjin was born in the city of Anyang, Gyeonggi Province of South Korea on December 4, 1992.
    Jin made his debut as a BTS member on June 13, 2013 with the release of BTS's first single album "2 Cool $ Skool." Addition to his role as a vocalist in BTS, Jin worked on following singles:


    1. "It's Definitely You" - with a fellow BTS member V - 2016
    2. "So Far Away" - with fellow BTS members SUGA and Jungkook - 2018
    3. "Tonight" - Jin's first solo single - June 4, 2019
    4. "Abyss" - Jin's second solo single - December 3, 2020
    5. "Yours" - a soundtrack for South Korean Drama "Jirisan" - October 2021
    6. "Super Tuna" - a special track - December 4, 2021

    Jin made his solo debut with his first single album "The Astronaut" on October 28, 2022. Following the releases of his mini albums Happy and Echo on November 15, 2024 and May 16, 2025 respectively.


  • SUGA aka. Min Yoongi

    SUGA, whose full name is Min Yoongi, is a rapper, songwriter, and producer under BigHit Music. He was born in Daegu, South Korea, on March 9, 1993.
    SUGA debuted as a BTS member on June 13, 2013, serving as one of the group's rappers and main producers. Known for his introspective lyrics and emotional depth, he also releases solo music under the name Agust D.


    1. "Agust D" - from his debut mixtape Agust D - August 15, 2016
    2. "Daechwita" - from his second mixtape D-2 - May 22, 2020
    3. "People Pt.2 (feat. IU)" - from his first solo album D-DAY - April 7, 2023
    4. "Haegeum" - title track of D-DAY - April 21, 2023

    SUGA's solo projects blend personal storytelling with social commentary, and he is credited with producing numerous BTS songs and tracks for other artists across genres.


  • J-Hope aka. Jung Hoseok

    j-hope debuted as a BTS member on June 13, 2013, known for his energetic performance style and bright personality. He also leads various BTS choreographies and has pursued a successful solo career.


    1. "Daydream" - from his first mixtape Hope World - March 2, 2018

  • Jimin aka. Park Jimin

    Jimin, whose full name is Park Jimin, is a vocalist, dancer, and songwriter under BigHit Music. He was born in Busan, South Korea, on October 13, 1995.
    Jimin debuted as a BTS member on June 13, 2013, and is celebrated for his expressive voice and graceful dance performances. He became the first BTS member to reach #1 on the Billboard Hot 100 as a soloist.


    1. "Lie" - from BTS album WINGS - October 10, 2016
    2. "Serendipity" - from Love Yourself: Her - September 18, 2017
    3. "Filter" - from MAP OF THE SOUL: 7 - February 21, 2020
    4. "Set Me Free Pt.2" - pre-release from his solo album FACE - March 17, 2023
    5. "Like Crazy" - title track from FACE - March 24, 2023

    Jimin's solo work highlights his artistic vulnerability and emotional intensity, earning him critical praise and multiple record-breaking achievements as a Korean solo artist.


  • V aka. Kim Taehyung

    V, whose full name is Kim Taehyung, is a vocalist, songwriter, and actor under BigHit Music. He was born in Daegu, South Korea, on December 30, 1995.
    V debuted as a BTS member on June 13, 2013, and is known for his deep baritone voice, vintage musical taste, and unique artistic sense that often bridges jazz, soul, and pop influences.


    1. "Stigma" - from BTS album WINGS - October 10, 2016
    2. "Singularity" - from Love Yourself: Tear - May 18, 2018
    3. "Scenery" - digital single - January 30, 2019
    4. "Sweet Night" - soundtrack for K-drama Itaewon Class - March 13, 2020
    5. "Love Me Again" - from his solo album Layover - August 9, 2023
    6. "Slow Dancing" - title track of Layover - September 8, 2023

    V's solo work captures a mellow, soulful aesthetic and has drawn acclaim for its cinematic production and emotional resonance.


  • Jungkook aka. Jeon Jungkook

    Jungkook, whose full name is Jeon Jungkook, is the main vocalist, dancer, and producer under BigHit Music. He was born in Busan, South Korea, on September 1, 1997.
    Jungkook debuted as a BTS member on June 13, 2013, and is recognized for his versatile vocal range and stage presence. He became the first BTS member to release a full-length English solo single.


    1. "Euphoria" - from BTS album Love Yourself: Answer - August 24, 2018
    2. "My Time" - from MAP OF THE SOUL: 7 - February 21, 2020
    3. "Stay Alive" - soundtrack for 7FATES: CHAKHO - February 11, 2022
    4. "Dreamers" - official FIFA World Cup Qatar 2022 soundtrack - November 20, 2022
    5. "Seven (feat. Latto)" - debut solo single - July 14, 2023
    6. "Standing Next to You" - title track from his solo album GOLDEN - November 3, 2023

    Jungkook's solo releases blend pop, R&B, and EDM influences, establishing him as one of the most globally recognized K-pop solo artists.
